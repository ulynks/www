What is it ?

CONNECT BY YERUN is a data-driven web application that recommends potential research collaborations and funding opportunities to each individual researcher in YERUN.

Our mission is to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange, sparking synergies among YERUN member institutions and paving the way for ground-breaking research outcomes. This initiative aims to return control to universities and the researchers themselves, enabling them to take a more active role in shaping their research agendas and funding pursuits.

Who is it for?

CONNECT BY YERUN is a platform developed for the researchers and research managers of the 23 universities that are part of the Young European Research Universities Network (YERUN).

All researchers with an ORCID ID can login but only the ones that are part of the 23 Universities will be able to take the most out of the platform.
